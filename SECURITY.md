# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Only the latest release on the `main` branch is supported. We do not backport fixes to older versions.

| Version | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| Latest  | Yes       |
| Older   | No        |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it responsibly. Do not open a public GitHub issue.

Send your report to **security@provii.app** with the following information:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof of concept code
- Your name and contact details (if you would like to be credited)

We will acknowledge receipt within two business days and provide an initial assessment within five business days.

## Coordinated Disclosure

We follow a 90-day coordinated disclosure window. After reporting a vulnerability, we ask that you:

- Allow us up to 90 days to investigate and remediate the issue before any public disclosure
- Work with us in good faith to understand the scope and impact
- Avoid exploiting the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it
- Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to other users

We will coordinate with you on the timing and content of any public advisory.

## Safe Harbour

We consider security research conducted in accordance with this policy to be authorised. We will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow this policy and act in good faith. If legal action is initiated by a third party against a researcher who has complied with this policy, we will take steps to make it known that the researcher's actions were conducted in accordance with this policy.

## Bug Bounty

We do not currently operate a bug bounty programme. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will credit researchers in security advisories where appropriate.
